Challenge 01 · Cybersecurity

Mitigating Severe Cybersecurity, Ransomware & Data Breach Risks

"Corpay electronically receives, processes, and stores highly sensitive data, including bank account information, social security numbers, and credit card details... systems are constantly targeted by bad actors using evolving techniques like ransomware and denial-of-service attacks."
— Corpay 2025 Form 10-K
Concert Dimensions

Concert Protect and Concert Secure Coder proactively identify, prioritize, and remediate vulnerabilities before they reach production.

1
Concert Protect

Concert Protect identifies and prioritizes CVEs based on actual business impact and exploitability within Corpay's specific environment. It evaluates the "blast radius" of each vulnerability across code, infrastructure, and runtime dependencies — ensuring the highest-risk issues are addressed first.

Concert Secure Coder

Embeds security directly into developers' workflows — scanning for vulnerabilities and exposed secrets while providing AI-assisted, automated remediations to fix vulnerable code before it reaches production. Concert also automates OS and middleware patching to address runtime vulnerabilities quickly.

  • Prioritize CVEs by real business impact, not just severity score
  • Fix vulnerable code before it reaches production
  • Automate OS and middleware patching
  • Reduce exposure window for ransomware and breach vectors

Challenge 02 · Reliability

Preventing System Outages & Software Defects

"Corpay's business relies heavily on the efficient and uninterrupted operation of interconnected computer systems... system outages, software defects, or errors during updates could result in transaction delays, financial losses, contractual penalties, and regulatory scrutiny."
— Corpay 2025 Form 10-K
Concert Dimensions

Concert Resilience, Concert Observe, and Concert Operate work together to prevent incidents and drastically reduce MTTR when they occur.

3
Concert Resilience

Maps signals from applications and infrastructure to evaluate system health against non-functional requirements — availability, scalability, and recoverability. Generates a resilience score and proactive recommendations to address gaps before they result in incidents.

Concert Observe + Concert Operate

Provides full-stack visibility and AI-driven automated runbooks to detect cascading failures, identify root cause, and orchestrate immediate remediation — drastically reducing Mean Time To Resolution (MTTR).

  • Identify resilience gaps before they cause outages
  • Detect cascading failures across interconnected systems
  • Automate remediation with AI-driven runbooks
  • Reduce MTTR and minimize transaction disruption

Challenge 03 · M&A

Taming IT Complexity from Mergers & Acquisitions

"Corpay relies on acquisitions to drive growth, recently acquiring Paymerang, Alpha, and AvidXchange. Integrating new businesses, technologies, and operations can result in unforeseen operating difficulties, increased costs, and diverted management attention."
— Corpay 2025 Form 10-K
Concert Capability

Concert acts as a unified control plane that connects insights across fragmented, siloed domains — without requiring a rip-and-replace of existing tools.

5

The Challenge

Each acquired company brings its own IT monitoring tools, infrastructure, and operational practices. Without a unifying layer, Corpay faces fragmented visibility, duplicated tooling costs, and governance gaps across its growing portfolio.

How Concert Solves It

Concert's Unified Entity Data Model creates a shared data layer that plugs newly acquired companies' varied IT tools directly into the platform — giving Corpay immediate, centralized visibility and governance over newly acquired infrastructure and applications without disrupting existing investments.

  • Connect acquired IT tools without rip-and-replace
  • Immediate centralized visibility post-acquisition
  • Shared data model across all Corpay entities
  • Reduce integration costs and management distraction

Challenge 04 · Compliance

Ensuring Compliance with Complex Global Regulations

"Corpay is subject to complex global regulations, including data privacy laws, anti-money laundering regulations, and PCI DSS. Failure to maintain compliance could lead to significant fines, intervention by card networks, and loss of business."
— Corpay 2025 Form 10-K
Concert Dimension

Concert Compliance evaluates application deployments against PCI, NIST, and SOC 2 frameworks with AI-powered automated remediation and an evidence store.

7
Concert Compliance

Evaluates application deployments against predefined security standards and frameworks — including PCI DSS, NIST, and SOC 2. Assesses compliance gaps, tracks posture trends over time, and provides automated watsonx-powered remediation steps for failed controls.

AI-Powered Evidence Store

Concert includes an evidence store where AI automatically evaluates submitted text or PDF evidence against controls to determine whether the proof of compliance is sufficient — reducing manual audit preparation effort significantly.

  • Continuous assessment against PCI, NIST, SOC 2
  • Track compliance posture trends over time
  • Automated remediation steps for failed controls
  • AI-evaluated evidence store reduces audit burden

Challenge 05 · Cost

Controlling Escalating IT & Cloud Costs

"Corpay invests heavily in its technology infrastructure, reporting approximately $408 million spent in 2025 on capital and operating expenses to operate and enhance their technology systems."
— Corpay 2025 Form 10-K
Concert Dimension

Concert Optimize (powered by IBM Turbonomic) continuously analyzes application demand to automatically manage performance and cost — preventing overprovisioning and containing cloud spend.

9
Concert Optimize · IBM Turbonomic

Concert Optimize continuously analyzes application demand alongside infrastructure and cloud signals to automatically manage performance and cost. It dynamically generates rightsizing recommendations and intelligent resource alignment to prevent overprovisioning.

Balancing Performance and Cost

Rather than choosing between application performance and cost governance, Concert Optimize balances both simultaneously — ensuring Corpay's $408M technology investment is allocated where it delivers the most value.

  • Continuous rightsizing recommendations
  • Prevent cloud overprovisioning automatically
  • Balance application performance with cost governance
  • Reduce waste across infrastructure and cloud spend
