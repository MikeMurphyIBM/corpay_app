Preparation

How to Prepare and What to Bring

This is a working session — please come prepared with real use cases related to real decisions and real data.

If you have use case ideas already in mind, prepare the following for each:

  • Process Flow Overview — A simple diagram or slide with clearly marked decision points.
  • Decision-Level Metrics — Transaction volume, associated revenue or cost, % manual review, error/fraud/exception rates, cycle times, rework, or satisfaction KPIs.
  • Known Pain Points — Bottlenecks, delays, inconsistent decisions, or sources of risk.
  • AI Ambition — Clear goals (e.g., reduce manual effort, improve accuracy, catch fraud earlier).
  • Data Readiness — Historical data availability and location, whether outcomes are labeled (e.g., approve/reject, fraud/clean), any quality gaps or concerns.

If you don't have a use case in mind, think about the following:

  • How are decisions made today? — Where are business rules used to make decisions? What are the areas where a faster, automated decision would bring business value?
  • New opportunities — What new opportunities exist in your workloads? (e.g., loss prevention, improved customer experience, fraud detection, reduced manual effort)
  • Existing AI implementation — Is AI being used off platform leveraging IBM Z data? Is any z/OS app sending in-transaction scoring requests off platform? What AI use cases have you implemented, and what is the relevance of IBM Z data in these?
